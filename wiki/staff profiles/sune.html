About Me

Hey there! I am SuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuunE, or simply SunE, one of the first staff members here at POS. You may know me as a host from countless events or a moderator for both iterations of the POMS (Planeguy7777 Official Minecraft Server). While I may not be very active in terms of messages sent, I'm constantly working behind the stage to keep the server running.

If you ever see me online, don't hesitate to say hello! If I don't respond I'm probably AFK or coding.

What I'm Proud Of:

On a fateful 2023 evening, a weary SunE sat down in front of his PC and logged on to Discord. Unfortunately, Jonathan was pulling a Jonathan and he painfully watched as every single channel vanished and all the users were banned. It was over for the POS... or was it?

I am proud of my work as staff in POS, not just for the quick events but for everything: from rebuilding the Discord server to integrating useful bots; most of the things one can see in POS was created or edited by me in the past for a greater outcome in the future. Today I not only work with my fellow staff members to help out with tickets, requests, Minecraft servers, and challenges, but strive to keep building upon this very server that was fallen a year ago.
